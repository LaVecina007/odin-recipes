Mojito Recipe

Mojito

Something I'd really like right now.

Minty fresh. This recipe is directly from All Recipes.

Ingredients

  • 6 mint leaves
  • 4 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 lime, cut into 6 wedges
  • 2 (1.5 fluid ounce) jiggers lemon-flavored rum
  • 1 cup ice cubes, or as needed
  • Â½ cup carbonated water, or as needed

Steps

  1. Put 3 mint leaves and 2 teaspoons sugar into each of 2 glass tumblers; vigorously stir sugar and mint together, crushing mint with the back of a spoon to release oils.
  2. Add 3 lime wedges to each glass; again stir vigorously to release some lime juice.
  3. Pour 1 jigger rum into each glass, then fill glasses with ice cubes. Top with carbonated water; stir.
